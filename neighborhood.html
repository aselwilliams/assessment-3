Rowlett, TX

My neighborhood is located in a secluded area on the border of the cities of Rowlett and Sachse. It has diverse community and cultural charm. There are shops, restaurants, a pharmacy, a gas station and a new hospital within close vacinity. What I like the most is that there is a walking trail and a gym.
